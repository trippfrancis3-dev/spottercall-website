Features

Your Gear. Your Data. Our Voice.

Import profiles from any ballistic app. Or pick from our built-in library. SpotterCall makes your data talk.

Voice Hold Calls

Call a distance in any increment. Hear the precise hold in MRAD or MOA instantly through your earbuds. No screen. No fumbling.

Import Any Profile

Bring your trued data from GeoBallistics, Applied Ballistics, Trasol, or any CSV. Your profiles. Your numbers. Our voice.

Reference Library

Don't have custom data? Pick your caliber, bullet, and manufacturer from our built-in library. Instant holds for factory ammo.

Shot Correction

Tell SpotterCall where you hit. It calculates the correction and calls the adjusted hold. Like having a spotter on the glass.

Live Weather

GPS-based atmospheric conditions. SpotterCall flags when temperature, pressure, or altitude shift enough to change your holds.

Any Earbuds

AirPods, Galaxy Buds, or any Bluetooth earbuds you already own. Pair and go. SpotterCall works with whatever's in your ear.

The Story

TRIPP & MIKE FRANCIS · BELZONI, MS · 1980

Mississippi · 1980 — Phoenix · 2025

Two brothers. One gun line.
Four decades of not missing.

"Time on the Trigger."

Tripp and Mike Francis grew up at the line in Belzoni, Mississippi — Humphreys County Junior 4-H District Champions before most kids had ever heard a shot break clean.

More than four decades later, still shooting competitively wherever the opportunity calls — from the gun line in Mississippi to anywhere in the nation. Still cleaning targets. Chasing Xs. No nines.
